Story

Gnosia is set in the far future, where humanity has become a massive spacefaring civilization, dealing with a threat called Gnosia, an infestation that compels people to murder other people in the name of their god, Gnos.

You managed to get a ride on a refugee ship escaping from a planet being ravaged by the Gnosia. However, during the trip to another planet, the ship's computer LeVi detects Gnosia infection on board the ship and following protocol, instructs the passengers and crew to deduce which one of them is the Gnosia and put them into cold sleep.

If they fail to do so, the ship is forced to self destruct.

Game Rules

Objective

Crew: Eliminate all Gnosia by voting them into Deep Freeze.

Gnosia: Eliminate crew members until you outnumber them.

Game Phases

1. Debate Phase

Discuss with other players who you think is Gnosia. Share information and suspicions.

Important: Crew members and helper roles must tell the truth. Only Gnosia are allowed and encouraged to lie.

2. Voting Phase

Vote to put one player into Deep Freeze. The player with the most votes is eliminated from the round.

No discussion allowed during this phase.

3. Warp Phase

During warp, the Gnosia select a crew member to eliminate, while helper roles perform their actions:

  • Gnosia: Choose a crew member to eliminate
  • Engineer: Investigate an alive player to learn if they're Human or Gnosia
  • Doctor: Investigate a dead player to learn their role (only with 2+ Gnosia)
  • Guardian: Protect a player from Gnosia elimination (cannot protect themselves)

No discussion allowed during this phase.

Once all actions are complete, the warp phase ends and the next round begins.

Roles

Crew Member

Standard crew with no special abilities. Work together to identify the Gnosia.

Gnosia

Imposters trying to eliminate the crew. Know who the other Gnosia are. Must eliminate crew members during the warp phase.

Engineer

Can investigate one alive player during warp phase to learn if they're Human or Gnosia.

Doctor

Can investigate one eliminated player during warp phase to learn their true role. Only available when there are 2 or more Gnosia.

Guardian

Can protect one player (except themselves) during warp phase from Gnosia elimination.

Tips

  • Helper roles (Engineer, Doctor, Guardian) are randomly assigned in equal numbers to the Gnosia count
  • Pay attention to voting patterns and player behavior
  • Engineers and Doctors should share their findings strategically
  • Gnosia can see each other but crew cannot
  • Discussion is only allowed during the Debate Phase
  • Remember: Crew and helper roles must be truthful, only Gnosia can deceive
